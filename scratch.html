Looking for some things to do in Cambodia?

  1. Places to visit in Phnom Penh:
    • Independence Monument - this is the heart of the city, located in the center of the biggest roundabout in Phnom Penh!
    • The Royal Palace - this Bhuddist-style palace is located along side the Mekong River and is lit up at night.
    • Wat Phnom - this monument is the also located near the Royal Palace and is a pagoda on top of the mountain.
    • Penguin Tower - this is the tallest building in all of cambodia and it even has a restaurant at the top!
    • Russian Market - this is the best market in Phnom Penh due to its wide variety of souvenirs and cheap prices.

  2. Places to visit in Siem Reab:
    • Angkor Wat Temple - this is the most famous site in Cambodia and is actually the largest religious structure in the world!
    • New Market - this market has everything from custom painted shirts to traditional scarfs called "grow-maas".
    • Pub Street - this popular tourist attraction is exactly what it sounds like: a party street every night.
    • Fish Massage - these places have the little tanks of fish where you put your feet and the fish eat the bacteria!

  3. Places to visit in Battambang:
    • Alligator Farms - carefully walk along the tops of skinny walls while live alligators swim below you...
    • Riverside Park Restaurants - looking for a cheap local meal or smoothie? Look no further than the Riverside Park!
    • The Bamboo Train - roughly an hour away from the city lives a bamboo train running around a beautiful mountain.
